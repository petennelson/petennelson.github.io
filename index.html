Pete Nelson, Entomologist

I’m an entomologist interested in arthropod-plant interactions, trophic interactions, biological control, and integrated pest management. I earned my BS and MS at Michigan State University and PhD at North Carolina State University. I am an independent crop consultant in Northwest Michigan with HortSystems.
